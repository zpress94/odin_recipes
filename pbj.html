Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhich

An All Time Favorite

This simple, yet stylish spin on an all time favorite meal, or snack (I don't judge) is one of the easiest to make. See the directions below.

What You'll Need

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter (crunchy or smooth, it's your choice)
  • Jelly (preferably grape, but you live your life how you need to)

What to do

  1. Grab 2 slices of bread
  2. Take a good whopping knife-full of peanut butter and spread it on one slice of bread
  3. Take a spoon full of jelly and smack it on the other slice
  4. Put bread slices together
  5. ENJOY
